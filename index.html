About Tanjiro

Tanjiro Komando is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps, who joined to find a remedy to turn his sister, Nezuko Kamado, back into a human and to hunt down and kill demons, and later swore to defeat Muzan Kibutsuji,the King of Demons, in order to prevent others from suffering the same fate as him.
